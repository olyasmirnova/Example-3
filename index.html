how we do it

preparation

First of all we get to know your product, your customers. Once we’re done, we look up targeting options that can work best for us and create a campaign plan and a content stragety.

creatives

Next we create copy and banners for each segment of our audience for your approval.

tech tricks

We create a campaign structure with various campaigns, ad sets and ads, define placements for our ads, choose bidding and optimization options, install Facebook tracking on your site.We also will help you set up with Google Analytics lead tracking.

Optimization

Once we launch our ads, we wait for them to perform a day or two and start optimizing. We test various targeting options, copy and visuals and stop worst performing ads.

Scaling up

When we have a well performing campaign, you might want to increase budget to get even more traffic. If done wrong, this may cause a drop in performance. We will make sure scaling goes smoothly for you.

analytics

We analyze performance statistics and track important business metrics like ROI and conversion costs. Based on that data we decide how to optimize and scale campaigns further.To loop you in we provide weekly reports on how’s your campaign doing.

FAQ

How do I pay?

Service fee consists of a monthly fixed price (paid in advance) and % of ad budget that you have spent (paid when the month is over). On top of that Facebook will charge you each time after a certain amount is owed on your account (usually couple times per week).

How much time does it take before I start collecting leads?

For that we allow 1 week after we confirm payment from you, although in most cases we try to finish all preparations in a few days.

How much ad budget should I allocate for start?

In most cases $150-$200 is enough to get first results and see your numbers like cost per click, cost per converson, ROI etc. Based on those numbers you and us will have a better understanding how much you need to spend henceforth.

Do you have a moneyback guarantee?

Yes, you are eligible for a full refund of your payment (excluding transaction fees) if you request moneyback in first 14 days after the transaction was completed.No questions asked.
